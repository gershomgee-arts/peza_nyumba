About Peza Nyumba

Reliable Student Support

Peza Nyumba was founded to bridge the gap between students and safe, affordable living environments. We understand the challenges of finding the right boarding house, which is why we verify every listing on our platform. Our mission is to make student life seamless and focused on what truly matters: your education.

Unified Campus Ecosystem

Beyond just housing, we provide an integrated ecosystem. From a community marketplace where you can buy second-hand textbooks to a grocery service that delivers essentials straight to your door. We are more than just a listing site; we are your ultimate campus companion.
